Aisle 5 is located in the historic 3320 Grand Ave Building

Built in 1917, 3320 Grand Ave was a free standing buildling pre-dating the construction of the Grand Lake Theater (1928)

The first recorded business was the Jenny Wren Spotless Foodstore

In deference to Jenny Wren, we called our pub Aisle 5

About Jenny Wren Spotless Foodstore

Jenny Wren was one of the very first grocery stores in the Bay Area

Their goal was to provide the highest-quality, fresh foods for the community at a reasonable price

Our mission is to provide a comfortable pub experience through a curated selection of outstanding craft beers, a sharable, beer-paired food menu, knowledgeable and friendly staff, and innovative service technology.
